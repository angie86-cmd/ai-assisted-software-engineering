# SMID Workshops Context

## Purpose of the Workshops

SMID workshops are practical training sessions designed to help participants use digital tools to create content, organize projects and generate real opportunities.

The workshops focus on practical application rather than passive learning.

## Typical Workshop Topics

Workshop topics may include:

- Canva
- Artificial Intelligence tools
- Digital content creation
- Business organization
- Communication
- Community empowerment
- Digital productivity

## Target Audience

The workshops are designed for people who:

- Are migrants
- Are entrepreneurs
- Are starting again in a new context
- Want to generate income
- Want to improve their content
- Need practical digital skills
- Feel they lose time creating content without clear results

## Workshop Format

Workshops may include:

- Live class
- Practical step-by-step content
- Downloadable material
- Participation in chat
- Final quiz
- Certificate of completion

## Access and Participation

Participants usually register through an online platform.

After registration, participants receive:

- Access to the learning platform
- Workshop information
- Class link
- Support channel or notification group

## Certification Criteria

To receive certification, participants may be expected to:

- Attend the live session
- Participate actively
- Keep the camera active when required
- Participate in the chat
- Complete the final quiz

## Important Constraints

Workshop access is limited to registered participants.

Scholarships may be available for participants who are committed to attending and completing the workshop.

## Relevance for SMID Compass

SMID Compass may support workshop guidance by:

- Explaining available workshops
- Helping users identify relevant workshop topics
- Providing registration guidance
- Explaining participation expectations
- Explaining certification requirements
- Routing workshop-related questions to a Workshop Advisor Agent
