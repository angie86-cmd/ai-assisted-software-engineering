# SMID Public Context

## Organization

Save My Identity (SMID) is an international non-profit organization focused on the human right to legal identity and proof of identity.

SMID supports people affected by lack of valid identity documents, especially in contexts of migration, forced displacement and statelessness.

## Mission

SMID works to make the lack of legal identity visible and to connect affected people with guidance, community support and international allies.

The organization understands legal identity not only as a documentation issue, but as a foundation for dignity, protection, mobility and access to rights.

## Core Problem

When people do not have valid identity documents, their access to basic rights can be seriously limited.

Affected areas include:

- Access to formal employment
- Access to healthcare
- Legal protection
- Mobility
- Family reunification
- Migration regularization
- Social participation

## Areas of Work

SMID works in several areas:

1. Legal identity and documentation
2. Passports and civil registration
3. Refugee and international protection information
4. Safe and regular migration
5. Digital identity and civic innovation
6. Community education and workshops
7. Public advocacy and international cooperation

## Human Rights Approach

SMID defends legal identity as a fundamental human right.

The organization provides general information and guidance from a human rights perspective and aims to protect dignity, inclusion and access to rights.

## Technology and Innovation

SMID explores ethical technology solutions for social impact, including:

- Digital identity
- AI-supported guidance
- Blockchain for human rights contexts
- Civic innovation
- Virtual assistants for informational support

## Important Boundaries

SMID does not replace public authorities, legal professionals or official institutions.

SMID provides general orientation, educational information and verified resources.
